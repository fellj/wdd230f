Galveston Island welcomed 7.3 million visitors in 2019. Visitation increased 2.1% in 2019 after registering 2.7% growth and 8.2% growth in 2018 and 2017, respectively. In 2019, 7.3 million visitors spent $883 million in Galveston Island, compared to 6.4 million visitors and $766 million in spending in 2015.

Wildlife surveys conducted around Galveston Bay since 2006 indicate that most finfish and bird populations appear to be maintaining, while many shellfish populations have been declining and require action to prevent further losses.
